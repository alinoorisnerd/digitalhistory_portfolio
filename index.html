About

Digital History - Course is targeted towards expanding:
1. Information Literacy - use of digital collections.
2. Digital Literacy - sharing scholarly work online.
3. Data Literacy - Collecting, processing, analyzing, and sharing data.
4. Computational Literacy - Use of Computational Methods.
This Website serves as a digital archive for Portfolio Activities 3, 4, and 5. In the spirit of historian-ship, future class discussion and points raised will be documented here on the website as well, as an extra endeavour. Additional Class related material such as slides , final project alongside visualized data will be included here as well.
